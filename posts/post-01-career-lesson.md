# Post 1 — Career Lesson: Mindset Shift

**Category:** Career Lesson / Mindset
**Status:** Posted
**Best Time to Post:** Monday 8–10 AM

---

## Post Text

Nobody told me this when I started in data analytics.

3 years in, I was writing complex SQL queries nobody asked for.
5 years in, I realized the real skill isn't writing queries.
It's knowing WHICH question to answer first.

Most analysts drown in dashboards.
The best analysts pause and ask: "What decision does this data need to support?"

That one shift changed everything for me.

→ Stop being a report factory.
→ Start being a decision partner.

The business doesn't need more data.
It needs better answers.
