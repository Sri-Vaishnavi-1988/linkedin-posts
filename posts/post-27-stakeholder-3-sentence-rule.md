## Post Text

The best analysts I know use the 3-sentence rule.

Before every stakeholder update, they answer:

Sentence 1: What happened?
"Revenue dropped 8% week-over-week."

Sentence 2: Why?
"It is driven by a 22% decline in mobile conversions, concentrated in the checkout step."

Sentence 3: What should we do?
"I recommend we A/B test the mobile payment flow — I can have the analysis ready by Thursday."

That's it. Three sentences.

Not a 20-slide deck.
Not a wall of text.
Not "here's the data, what do you think?"

Executives make decisions at sentence 3.
They don't need to understand your methodology.
They need to trust your recommendation.

Give them that.

Try this format in your next stakeholder email.
Then come back and tell me what happened. 👇
