## Post Text

I reviewed 50+ Data Analyst resumes last month.

Here's what separates the ones that get interviews from the ones that don't:

❌ "Responsible for creating dashboards"
✅ "Built 3 executive dashboards in Power BI tracking  revenue pipeline, reducing weekly reporting time by 4 hours"

❌ "Used SQL to query databases"
✅ "Wrote optimized SQL queries in Snowflake across 50M+ row datasets, improving report load time by 60%"

❌ "Worked with stakeholders"
✅ "Partnered with Sales and Finance teams to define KPIs for a new product launch, influencing  budget allocation"

The formula is simple:
Action verb + Tool/Skill + Scale/Context + Business Impact

Recruiters spend 7 seconds on your resume.
Make every bullet earn its spot.

Save this. Your next resume will thank you. 🔖
