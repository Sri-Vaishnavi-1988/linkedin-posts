## Post Text

Hot take: Excel is still the most powerful analytics tool in 2025.

I know. I know.

The data community will come for me.

But hear me out.

SQL is faster for querying.
Python is better for automation.
Power BI/Tableau makes prettier dashboards.

But Excel is the ONE tool:
→ Every stakeholder already has
→ Every manager already understands
→ That lets you prototype an analysis in 10 minutes

I've seen  decisions made in Excel pivot tables.
I've also seen beautiful Snowflake pipelines that nobody uses.

The best tool is the one that gets used and trusted.

Don't abandon Excel.
Master it AND modern tools.

Disagree? Tell me why 👇
