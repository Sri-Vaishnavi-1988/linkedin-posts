## Post Text

Tableau vs Power BI.

I've used both professionally. Here's my honest take:

POWER BI
✅ Best if your company is Microsoft-first
✅ Deeper DAX = more flexible calculations
✅ Better for enterprise, governed reporting
✅ More affordable at scale
❌ Desktop app can feel clunky
❌ Sharing requires Power BI Pro licenses

TABLEAU
✅ Best-in-class data visualization flexibility
✅ Intuitive drag-and-drop for exploration
✅ Better for ad-hoc analysis and storytelling
✅ Tableau Public = free portfolio builder
❌ Expensive
❌ Slower with very large datasets

My verdict:
→ If your company uses Azure/Microsoft 365 → Power BI
→ If you're building a portfolio or doing deep visual analytics → Tableau
→ Best analysts? Learn both.

The tool matters less than your ability to tell a story with it.

Which do you prefer and why? 👇
