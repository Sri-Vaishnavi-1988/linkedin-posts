## Post Text

5 skills that took me from Junior to Senior Data Analyst faster than any certification:

1. Stakeholder Communication
   Not "the data shows X."
   But "based on this, I recommend Y because Z."

2. SQL Window Functions
   RANK(), LAG(), LEAD(), ROW_NUMBER()
   These 4 functions solve 80% of complex business questions.

3. Data Storytelling
   Build dashboards that guide decisions, not just display numbers.

4. Business Acumen
   Understand P&L, CAC, LTV, churn.
   Speak finance. Earn a seat at the table.

5. Documentation Habits
   Write down what every metric means.
   Future you (and your team) will be grateful.

Certifications prove you studied.
These skills prove you deliver.

Which one are you working on right now? 👇
