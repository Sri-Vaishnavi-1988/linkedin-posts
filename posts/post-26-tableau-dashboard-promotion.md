## Post Text

This Tableau dashboard got me noticed by leadership.

Here's exactly what I built and why it worked.

The situation:
Sales team was manually pulling numbers every Friday.
The VP was making decisions from week-old data.
Nobody knew which region was falling behind in real time.

What I built:
→ Page 1: Executive Summary — 3 KPIs, traffic light status, trend arrow
→ Page 2: Regional Breakdown — map + bar chart, drill-down by city
→ Page 3: At-Risk Accounts — table of accounts below target with % gap

What made it different:
→ It told a story, not just showed numbers
→ Page 1 could be understood in 10 seconds
→ The VP could answer "where are we losing?" in under 2 clicks

The result:
Friday manual pulls stopped.
The VP shared it in the board meeting.
I was asked to present the methodology to the wider analytics team.

The technical skills got me to the starting line.
The storytelling crossed the finish line.

What dashboard changed your visibility at work? 👇
