## Post Text

Recruiters aren't searching for "Data Analyst."

They're searching for:
→ "SQL Power BI Data Analyst"
→ "Snowflake dbt Analytics Engineer"
→ "Python Tableau Business Intelligence"

Your LinkedIn headline is your SEO.

I see so many great analysts with headlines like:
"Data Analyst at [Company]"

That's a job title. Not a brand.

Try this instead:
"Senior Data Analyst | SQL · Python · Power BI | Turning Data into Decisions"

You just went from invisible to searchable.

3 things to optimize today:
1. Headline → keywords + value prop
2. About section → what problems you solve
3. Skills section → every tool you actually use

Recruiters are searching RIGHT NOW.
Make sure they can find you.
