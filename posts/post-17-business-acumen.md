## Post Text

The fastest way to get promoted as a Data Analyst?

Stop speaking data. Start speaking business.

What I mean:

Junior Analyst: "The conversion rate dropped 12% last week."

Senior Analyst: "The conversion rate dropped 12% last week. Based on the cohort analysis, it's concentrated in mobile users from paid search — which is costing us roughly  in lost monthly revenue. I'd recommend A/B testing the mobile checkout flow."

Same data. Same query.
Completely different career trajectory.

The difference is business acumen.

3 ways to build it:
1. Sit in on finance and sales meetings (even if not invited... especially if not invited)
2. Learn your company's top 5 KPIs and WHY they matter
3. Before every analysis ask: "What decision will this data influence?"

Data skills get you in the door.
Business acumen gets you to the table.

What's the most business-impactful analysis you've done? 👇
