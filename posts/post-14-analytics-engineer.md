## Post Text

The role nobody talked about 3 years ago is now one of the hottest in data:

Analytics Engineer.

What is it?
→ Part Data Analyst (understands business questions)
→ Part Data Engineer (builds reliable pipelines)
→ The bridge between raw data and trusted insights

Core skills:
→ SQL (advanced)
→ dbt (data transformation)
→ Snowflake or BigQuery
→ Git & version control
→ Data modeling

Why it pays well:
Because most companies have analysts who can answer questions
AND engineers who can move data.
Very few people who can do BOTH.

That gap is worth –+ in most markets.

If you're a Data Analyst who's comfortable in SQL and wants to level up your career without becoming a full software engineer → Analytics Engineering is your path.

Are you an Analytics Engineer or working toward it? 👇
