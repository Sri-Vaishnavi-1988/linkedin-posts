## Post Text

A day in my life as a Senior Data Analyst (the real version, not the LinkedIn version):

8:30 AM — Coffee. Open Slack. 3 "urgent" requests waiting. None are urgent.

9:00 AM — Pull data in Snowflake. Query takes 4 minutes. I refresh it 7 times anyway.

10:00 AM — Stakeholder meeting. They want "a quick dashboard." Nothing is ever quick.

11:30 AM — Discover a data quality issue from 3 months ago. Fun.

12:00 PM — Lunch. Spend it thinking about the data quality issue.

1:00 PM — Fix the data quality issue. It was a timezone offset. Classic.

2:00 PM — Write Python script to automate a report I've built manually 20 times.

4:00 PM — Present analysis. Stakeholder says "Can we see this by region too?" I say yes and quietly update the dbt model.

5:30 PM — Close 6 browser tabs of SQL queries I'll "come back to."

5:31 PM — Open one more.

The chaos is real. So is the impact.

What does YOUR data day look like? 👇
